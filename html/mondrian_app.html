MONDRIAN APP

Design and concept of an interactive application with the subject Piet Mondrian.

In a playful way the user can learn about his style and try to abstract a painting himself. Moreover he can discover information about Mondrain as an artist, his life and De Stijl.

The whole design keeps close to Mondrians colours, forms and abstract ways. The main menu as well as the information graphics in the second part are generated from one of Mondrains paintings. Those transform a two-dimensional avant-garde painting into a three-dimensional interactive infographic.
